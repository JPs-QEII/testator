Simple Will Generator

Generates a basic will based on the Queensland Law Handbook model will, for a single executor and a sole beneficiary. This tool runs entirely in your browser — nothing you type is sent anywhere.

This is a simple template intended for straightforward situations only. It is not legal advice. If your circumstances are more complex (multiple beneficiaries, trusts, minor children, blended families, etc.) you should consult a solicitor.
