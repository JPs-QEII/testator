Simple Will Generator

Generates a basic will based on the Queensland Law Handbook model will. This tool runs entirely in your browser — nothing you type is sent anywhere.

This is a simple template intended for straightforward situations only. It is not legal advice.

First, a few questions

Will you appoint a single executor, or joint executors?
Will this will establish a trust (for example, a testamentary trust)?
Do you have any minors (children under 18) in your care?
Do you have any pets or other animals in your care?
Will your entire estate go to a single beneficiary?

This tool doesn't support wills that establish a trust. Testamentary trusts need drafting tailored to your situation — please consult a solicitor.

This tool doesn't support that combination of answers yet. Guardianship for minors, care arrangements for pets, and multiple beneficiaries are all coming in a future update. For now, this tool only handles an estate with no trust, no minors or pets in your care, going to a single beneficiary.

If that doesn't match your situation, please consult a solicitor. Otherwise, go back and check your answers.
